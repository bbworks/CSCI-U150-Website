Iron Cross Band Page




Iron Cross is a Simpsonville-based Christian rock band from South Carolina. Formerly "The Rising Swords", formed in 2009, classmates and best friends guitarist Jackson Bailey and guitarist/keyboardist Bradley Biera discovered their identical talent on the electric guitar. Accompanied by guitarist Brandon Biera, the trio had their first band practice Friday, December 11, 2009.
